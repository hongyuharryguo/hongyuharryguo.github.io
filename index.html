Hongyu (Harry) Guo

Email: cxiao34@wisc.edu

I am Hongyu Guo, an assistant professor at the University of Wisconsin, Madison and a research scientist at NVIDIA .

I am currently very interested in exploring the safety and security of Large Language Model Systems and studying the role of LLMs in different application domains.


I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University.
