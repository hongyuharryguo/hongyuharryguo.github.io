Hongyu (Harry) Guo

Email: hongyu.guo@uottawa.ca

I am a Senior Researcher Officer at the Digital Technologies Research Center of the National Research Council Canada (NRC) . I am also an Adjunct Professor in the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Ottawa. I received the BEng and PhD degrees from the Shanghai Jiao Tong University and University of Ottawa, respectively, both in computer science. Alternative contact email: hongyu.guo@nrc-cnrc.gc.ca

I am very interested in deep learning and its applications in NLP, vision, graphs, and drug/material discovery.
